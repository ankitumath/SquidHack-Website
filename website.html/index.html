THE GAME EXPLAINED

Squid Hack 2026 is a premier 24-hour hackathon designed to test your technical limits. Organized by the SCYP CLUB, this event brings together the brightest minds to innovate, solve complex real-world problems, and survive the ultimate coding challenge.

Will you play it safe, or will you risk it all to build something extraordinary? The clock is ticking, and the massive prize pool awaits the ultimate survivors.

RULES OF THE GAME

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

◯ Who can participate?

Any student currently enrolled in a university degree program can participate. You do not need to be a Computer Science major to play the game!

△ What is the allowed team size?

You can participate individually or in a team of up to 4 members. We highly encourage forming diverse teams to combine different skills for survival.

◻ Is there any registration fee?

Yes, a 100rs team registraion fee is required then after the shortlisting by PPT selection team have to fill a form of worth 1500 includes your food and accommodation.
